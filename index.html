Welcome to TCE

Founded in 1957 by philanthropist and industrialist Late Shri.Karumuttu Thiagarajan Chettiar, Thiagarajar College of Engineering (TCE) is a Government Aided Institution and affiliated to Anna University and situated in Madurai, the Temple City .The college is funded by Central and State Governments and Management. The courses offered in TCE are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and Council of Architecture (COA), New Delhi. TCE was granted Autonomy in the year 1987 and the programmes have been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).TCE offers 11 Undergraduate programmes,7 Postgraduate programmes and Doctoral programmes in Engineering, Architecture and Science.

Founder

Kalaithanthai Karumuttu Thiagarajan Chettiar, is a great visionary with a mission. He was bestowed with the moral and social responsibility of building up this institution with an objective of serving the society. A staunch nationalist and a great industrialist, he led a life of sacrifice. He was as an exceptional educationalist, who had a keen eye on transforming lives through education. As an exceptional social savant, he left his footprints in the sands of time in all facets of his social life. Being a discrete reader, his passion for language was evident as he possessed a great command over the language of Tamil. As a generous philanthropist through his righteous deeds, he continues to live in the hearts of many. Kalaithanthai Karumuttu Thiagarajan Chettiar is a holistic personality with spiritual aura and soul.

Emeritus Chairman

Dr. Mrs. Radha Thiagarajan was the first and two-times Vice Chancellor of Alagappa University. She is gifted with the gab and considered to be an eminent multi-lingual scholar. She is acclaimed to be one of the most widely respected academicians with sound knowledge and intelligence. She rendered her service to society by being a member of several prestigious bodies. With a fervour for language and literature, she is a staunch patron for religious and literary scholars. All walks of her life she continued to carry herself as an epitome of love and care, through her generous contribution for noble causes. Today she is revered and admired as a respected mother-figure by many.

Former Chairman and Correspondent

Mr. Karumuttu T.Kannan, Chairman & Correspondent of Thiagarajar College of Engineering, holds the prestigious post as a Member of the Tamil Nadu State Planning Commission, Chennai. He is also the Member of the Planning Board of Madurai Kamaraj University. Inbuilt with values and principles, he is a visionary leader who has relentless desire to serve the humanity. As a dynamic industrialist and a seasoned educationalist, he has to his credit an experience of over 30 years in college administration. He has proved himself to be an able Entrepreneurial leader and philanthropist by his inimitable ability to steer an institution to greater heights. His thirst for arts and literature has kept himself as a custodian of Indian culture, arts, traditions and heritage.

Chairman and Correspondent

K. Hari Thiagarajan was born on 2nd August, 1988, and is the son of Sri. & Smt. Karumuttu T. Kannan and grandson of Sri & Smt. Karumuttu Thiagarajan Chettiar. Graduated from the University of Warwick, UK with a degree in Engineering Management. Managing Director of Thiagarajar Mills (P) Ltd., Madurai. Chairman & Managing Director of VTM Limited, Virudhunagar. Secretary of Thiagarajar College [Arts & Science], Madurai and Chairman & Correspondent of Thiagarajar College of Engineering,Madurai. Past Chairman of CII (Confederation of Indian Industry), Tamil Nadu State Council. Member of Entrepreneurs’ Organization, Chennai Chapter. Executive Committee Member of SIMA (The Southern India Mills’ Association), Coimbatore. He is passionate about classical Tamil literature & Sanskrit. His other interests include photography, reading, cycling & mentoring potential entrepreneurs.
